Hero

Spider-Man

First Appearance: Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962)

Bitten by a radioactive spider, shy teenagager, Peter Parker, gains the proportional speed and strength of an arachnid.
Spider-Man's greatest villains include The Green Goblin, Doctor Octopuss, and Venom.
Peter Parker's love interests include Gwen Stacy, Mary Jane Watson, and Cindy Moon.
